EpiWorks

  • Category: Cancer, Hackathon
  • Project date: February 5 2022
  • Slide deck: Click here

A new solution for predicting the growth of medulloblastoma before it becomes an issue

This is a project that my team came up with in a 24-hour time frame at a hackathon as part of TKS. We explored the root causes of medulloblastoma, a rare but devastating childhood brain tumour, and discovered its epigenetic causes. We then identified that certain patterns exist within methylation on the DNA of tumour cells, and figured out that data from epigenetic tests could be used to predict the future growth of this tumour solely from the methylation data within the cerebrospinal fluid.
